*MESSAGE 1: INTRODUCTION*

*🚛 SMART HAULIERS SYSTEM - Business Proposal*

We're proposing a new feature that will *automatically identify and select the correct haulier and billing partner* when a vehicle's registration number is captured during weighing operations.

*Key Benefits:*
✅ 80% reduction in data entry time
✅ Near-zero selection errors
✅ Faster weighing process
✅ Accurate invoicing automatically

*MESSAGE 2: CURRENT VS PROPOSED*

*📋 CURRENT PROCESS (Manual):*

1. Operator captures numberplate
2. Manually searches for haulier (50+ options)
3. Manually searches for business partner (30+ options)
4. Completes transaction
⏱️ *Time: 2-3 minutes per vehicle*

❌ *Problems:*
• Time-consuming during busy periods
• Risk of selecting wrong haulier/partner
• Inconsistent data entry
• Invoice errors

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

*📋 PROPOSED PROCESS (Automated):*

1. Operator captures numberplate
2. ✅ *System auto-selects haulier*
3. ✅ *System auto-selects business partner*
4. Operator verifies and continues
5. Completes transaction
⏱️ *Time: 15 seconds per vehicle*

✅ *Benefits:*
• Faster weighing process
• Correct selections automatically
• Consistent data entry
• Accurate invoicing
